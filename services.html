How does the site work?




The first rule

In order to put your car on our website, it is very easy. You only have to contact us and send pictures of your car and all the details related to it and make your symbolic financial payment.

The second rule

In order to appear in the first three places or at the top of each region of the Kingdom of Morocco on our website, you must pay any amount in addition to the main amount, and we evaluate the amounts and see who is the most and put them in the first places..

The third rule

If two months have passed since your car was listed, the post will be deleted, unless you want to pay again and continue with us.

The fourth rule

In order to list your car on our site you must pay just 20 dhs for two months.
